Who is Sam Deaver?

Born in 2003, Sam Deaver has been a resident of the city of Springfield, MO, for all of his life. Showing an aspiration for computers and how they work as early as elementary school, he took up a couple of classes concerning digital communication in high school, and enrolled in the Ozarks Technical Community College to learn about software development and website development. His biggest hobby is that of reviewing music, where he studies the impact of different works over the years and compiles his favorites.

Currently, he has worked on a handful of projects, and is awaiting to publish his most anticipated one. He's done collaborative work with other OTC students, and has assisted in the creation of a fully fledged mockup of a company website.
